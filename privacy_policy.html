Twitter/X Account Login and Cookies

Purpose of Login: Our app offers an optional feature to log in to your Twitter / X account. This feature is strictly optional and is only required if you wish to download content that is marked as "sensitive," "age-restricted," or "private" by Twitter (content that you are otherwise authorized to view). Public videos can be downloaded without logging in.

No Credential Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit your Twitter username or password. The login process happens directly on Twitter's secure website via an embedded browser view.

Cookie Usage: When you log in successfully, the app saves your authentication cookies (specifically `auth_token` and `ct0`) locally on your device.

  • Local Storage Only: These cookies are stored securely on your device's internal storage and are never sent to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Usage: The app uses these cookies solely to authenticate requests sent directly to Twitter's API to fetch the video data you requested. This allows the app to "act as you" to download the content you have access to.
